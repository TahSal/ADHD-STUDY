Through ADHD Eyes

Exploring and Improving ADHD Adults' Healthcare Experiences in The Netherlands Using Photovoice and Human-Centered Design

Join Our Study

About the Project

This innovative research study integrates Photovoice methodology with Human-Centered Design to explore and improve the experiences of adults with ADHD in healthcare settings.

By empowering participants to document their experiences through photographs and stories, we aim to foster a deeper understanding of the challenges they face and develop more effective solutions.

Adults with ADHD face unique challenges when navigating the healthcare system. These challenges are often invisible or misunderstood by healthcare providers, leading to negative experiences and inadequate addressing of these individuals' needs.

Study Aims

This research aims to:

  • Understand the experiences of individuals with ADHD in healthcare settings through their own lens
  • Identify challenges and barriers they face when accessing healthcare
  • Explore the strengths and resources they use to navigate the healthcare system
  • Develop innovative, human-centered solutions to improve healthcare experiences
  • Empower participants to be active agents of change in improving healthcare services

Study Method

Our integrated methodology consists of five key phases over 2 months:

  • Phase 1: Empathy and Preparation (2 weeks)
  • Phase 2: Exploration and Documentation (3-4 weeks) - Photo capture and reflection
  • Phase 3: Analysis and Definition (2 weeks) - Collaborative analysis
  • Phase 4: Co-Design and Prototyping (2 weeks) - Solution development
  • Phase 5: Engagement and Impact (2 weeks) - Photovoice exhibition and stakeholder dialogue

Participants will capture 15-20 photos documenting their healthcare experiences and participate in group discussions to develop solutions.

Why Participate?

By joining this study, you will:

  • Have your voice heard and experiences validated
  • Contribute to improving healthcare for people with ADHD
  • Learn new skills in photography and design thinking
  • Connect with others who share similar experiences
  • Help create practical solutions for real healthcare challenges
  • Participate in a Photovoice exhibition to share your story

Eligibility: Adults (18+) with ADHD diagnosis living in The Netherlands
