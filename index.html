Research Overview

Our Mission

We are pioneering an innovative approach to understanding and improving ADHD healthcare experiences in The Netherlands. By combining the power of visual storytelling through Photovoice with Human-Centered Design principles, we aim to amplify the voices of adults with ADHD and create meaningful change in healthcare delivery.

This research addresses the critical gap between healthcare providers' understanding and the lived experiences of adults with ADHD, fostering empathy and driving systemic improvements.

Research Objectives

  • Capture authentic healthcare experiences through participant-led photography
  • Identify systemic barriers and opportunities for improvement
  • Co-create evidence-based solutions with stakeholders
  • Empower participants as advocates for healthcare transformation
  • Develop actionable recommendations for healthcare providers
  • Create lasting impact through community engagement and policy influence

Participation Information

Why Participate?

  • Share your story and have your voice heard by healthcare decision-makers
  • Contribute to meaningful improvements in ADHD healthcare services
  • Develop new skills in photography, storytelling, and design thinking
  • Connect with a supportive community of individuals with shared experiences
  • Participate in creating evidence-based solutions for real healthcare challenges
  • Showcase your experiences through a public Photovoice exhibition

Eligibility Requirements

  • Adults aged 18 years or older
  • Diagnosed with ADHD by a qualified healthcare professional
  • Currently residing in The Netherlands
  • Comfortable using a camera or smartphone for photography
  • Willing to participate in group discussions and workshops
  • Available for the full research period

Language: Sessions will be conducted in English with Dutch translation support available when needed.

What's Involved

  • Orientation Workshop: Introduction to research methods and photography techniques
  • Photo Documentation: Capture your healthcare experiences over several weeks
  • Reflection Sessions: Individual and group discussions about your photographs
  • Co-Design Workshops: Collaborative solution development sessions
  • Exhibition Preparation: Curate your work for public presentation
  • Impact Activities: Engage with stakeholders and policymakers

Time Commitment: Flexible participation over 2 months with approximately 20-25 hours total involvement.
