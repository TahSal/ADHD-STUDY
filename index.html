Through ADHD Eyes

Mapping the Adult ADHD Healthcare Journey in the Netherlands

Photovoice Participatory Action Research

Study Introduction

Adults with ADHD often face long waits, sensory-busy clinics, and complex paperwork in Dutch healthcare. Through ADHD Eyes invites 5–6 adults to photograph these moments, highlight barriers and bright spots, and design quick “Journey Boosters” clinics can adopt. The Neurodiversity Foundation leads this eight-week participatory action research: every participant is a co-researcher shaping real change.

Study Aim

  • Document barriers and facilitators during help-seeking, waiting, and consultation.
  • Co-create and pilot low-cost Journey Boosters for ADHD-friendly clinics.
  • Produce a Neuro-Inclusive Care Pledge informed by lived experience.

Method in 3 Steps

  1. Photovoice capture – six photos per participant showing good & bad moments.
  2. Reflection workshop – group discussion to spot themes & design boosters.
  3. Booster test & pledge – try fixes, sign a Neuro-Inclusive Care Charter.
